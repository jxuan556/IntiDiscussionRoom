Opening Hours: PLEASE TAKE NOTE OF THE OPENING HOURS OF DISCUSSION ROOM IS ON MONDAYS - FRIDAYS: 8.00am - 6.00pm. THEN, SATURDAYS, SUNDAYS, PUBLIC HOLIDAYS: CLOSED

Discussion Rooms: There are five discussion rooms available for discussion.

Rules and Regulations

  1. Maximum usage of the room is two hours for each group.
  2. Discussion room reservations must be made on the day itself.
  3. Usage of the discussion room is according to maximum seating allowed. If the number exceeds the capacity, kindly inform the librarians at the counter.
  4. Sleeping is prohibited in the discussion room.
  5. All the discussion rooms are being monitored by CCTV. Any groups that misuse the discussion rooms will be barred immediately.
  6. Any maintenance issues with INTI properties should be reported immediately to the librarians.
  7. Please make sure that the discussion rooms are kept clean at all times.
